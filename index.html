Sickle Cell Programme Data Science Kikapu

This is Sickle Cell Programme Data Science Kikapu where Data Science Team collaborate for the day to day activities. Kikapu is the Swahili word which means Basket, so all Data Science activities are collected in the Kikapu for collaboration.
